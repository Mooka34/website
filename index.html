Things to Do in Delaware

Delaware Riverfront

The Delaware Riverfront is a revitalized area offering a mix of dining, entertainment, and cultural experiences. Visitors can enjoy a stroll along the scenic riverwalk, explore museums, or catch a show at local theaters. The area also hosts various festivals and events throughout the year, making it a lively destination.

Visit the Delaware Riverfront Website

Christiana Mall

The Christiana Mall is one of Delaware's premier shopping destinations, featuring over 200 stores, dining options, and a movie theater.

Visit the Christiana Mall Website

Duffy's Hope Celebrity Basketball Game

The 18th Annual Duffy's Hope Celebrity Basketball Game, held on August 3th, 2024, at Chase Fieldhouse in Wilmington, DE, features celebrities like Mani Love, DC Young Fly, Bobbe Thompson, and Young Dylan. Delaware Beaches

Rehoboth Beach

Rehoboth Beach is known for its boardwalk, charming shops, and dining options. Visitors can relax on the sandy shores, swim in the ocean, or stroll along the boardwalk.

Activities:

  • Beach Relaxation: Sunbathe, swim, and build sandcastles.
  • Boardwalk Fun: Visit Funland amusement park, play games at the arcades, and enjoy local treats like saltwater taffy.
  • Dining and Shopping: Explore restaurants, cafes, and unique shops along the boardwalk and Rehoboth Avenue.

Dewey Beach

Dewey Beach is known for its lively nightlife and watersports activities.

Activities:

  • Watersports: Try kayaking, paddleboarding, or jet skiing.
  • Nightlife: Enjoy live music, beach bars, and clubs.

Bethany Beach

Bethany Beach offers a family-friendly atmosphere with a quieter beach scene and a charming downtown area.

Activities:

  • Beach Activities: Perfect for families, with swimming, surfing, and beach games.
  • Boardwalk: A smaller boardwalk ideal for peaceful walks, with shops and eateries.

Fenwick Island

Fenwick Island is known for its peaceful beaches and natural beauty.

Activities:

  • Outdoor Adventures: Visit Fenwick Island State Park for swimming, sunbathing, and fishing.
  • Mini Golf and Water Parks: Mini-golf and water park fun at Viking Golf & Go-Karts and Thunder Lagoon Waterpark.

Getting There

From Philadelphia International Airport:

Take the SEPTA R7 train directly to Wilmington Station. From there, use DART First State transportation to reach your destination, whether it be a hotel, the Christiana Mall, or the Riverfront.

For more information on bus routes and schedules, visit the DART First State Website.
